final : Final is used to apply restrictions on class, method and variable.
 Final class can't be inherited,final method can't be overridden and final
 variable value can't be changed.
-final is a keyword.

finally: Finally is used to place important code, it will be executed 
whether exception is handled or not.
-finally is a block.

finalize: Finalize is used to perform clean up processing just before
 object is garbage collected.
-finalize is a method.
